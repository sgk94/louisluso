# Q-Vision Optics — Business Plan Summary

## Company Overview

- **Legal entity:** Q-Vision Optics Incorporated
- **Brand:** Louisluso (louisluso.com)
- **Founded:** 2011 in Chicago, IL — launched operations 2012
- **HQ:** Arlington Heights, IL
- **EU subsidiary:** Leicester, UK (est. 2016, 5-person sales team)
- **Manufacturing:** OEM partnerships with South Korean producers
- **Markets:** U.S., Canada, Europe

## Core Differentiator

First to introduce **ULTEM (Polyetherimide/PEI)** to the U.S. optical market — an aerospace/medical-grade material offering durability, flexibility, and lightweight comfort. 10+ years of expertise with this material.

## Product Portfolio

All collections use ULTEM material under the Louisluso brand:

| Collection | Target |
|---|---|
| Classic Series | Core line |
| Junior Series | Youth |
| Signature Series | Premium |
| Signature Plus Series | Premium+ |
| London Series | European-inspired |

## Current Distribution

- **Target segment:** Independent optical retailers (~35,000 stores in U.S.)
- **Active partnerships:** 800+ independent optical stores
- **Sales reps:** 5 reps covering 31 states
- **Exclusive dealers:** 2 (one U.S., one Canada)
- **Retail:** 1 company-owned store in Palatine, IL

## Market Opportunity

- U.S. vision care market: **$76.5B** (2022)
- Optical retail segment: **$23B**, growing at **5.5%/year**
- Key trend: growing demand for **mid-priced, high-quality** frames — consumers moving away from both cheap imports and overpriced premium brands
- Q-Vision sits squarely in this sweet spot
