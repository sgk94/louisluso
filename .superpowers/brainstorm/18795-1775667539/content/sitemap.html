Sitemap & Page Breakdown

Every page on the new site — carried over from current site + new features

Public Pages (no login required)

/ — Homepage
Hero banner, collection showcases, brand intro, CTA to browse or become a partner
/collections — All Collections
Grid of collection cards (Classic, Junior, Signature, London, Milan, Urban, Skylite, etc.)
/collections/[slug] — Collection Page
Product grid for a specific collection. Filters by color. Shows SRP pricing.
/products/[slug] — Product Detail
Product images, color variants, SRP price, specs (material, size, weight). "Find Nearest Dealer" button. Logged-in B2B users see bespoke pricing + "Add to Quote" button.
/why-louisluso — Why LOUISLUSO?
ULTEM material info, FDA/ECO compliance, brand differentiation
/about — About Us
Mission, vision, values, company story
/find-a-dealer — Dealer Locator
Map with address/radius search. Pulls active dealers from Zoho CRM. "Contact This Dealer" sends email with product info + customer details.
/become-a-partner — Partnership Application
Form: company, contact name, email, phone, address. Submits to Zoho CRM as a Lead.
/contact — Contact Us
Contact form + Google Maps embed (Q-Vision Optics, Arlington Heights, IL)
/appointment — Book an Appointment
Date/time picker for scheduling a meeting with Ken

B2B Portal (login required — Clerk)

/portal — Dashboard
Welcome screen, recent orders, account summary, quick actions
/portal/catalog — B2B Catalog
Same products but with bespoke wholesale pricing. "Add to Quote" cart functionality with count badge.
/portal/quote — Current Quote / Cart
Review items, quantities, bespoke pricing. Submit as order → creates Sales Order in Zoho Books.
/portal/orders — Order History
Past orders, invoice status, payment status. Pulled from Zoho Books.
/portal/account — Account Settings
Company info, shipping address, contact details. Managed via Clerk + synced to Zoho CRM.
